<p><strong>The Wireline Logging Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cased Hole Wireline Logging Services</li><li>Open Hole Wireline Logging Services</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireline logging services market is categorized into two main types: cased hole and open hole wireline logging services. Cased hole logging involves evaluating wells after they have been completed and cemented, providing insights into formation properties and potential production. Open hole logging is conducted in newly drilled wells before casing is installed, offering data on formation characteristics and fluid content. Both services are essential for optimizing hydrocarbon recovery and ensuring efficient reservoir management in the oil and gas industry.</p></p>
