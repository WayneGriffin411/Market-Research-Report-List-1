<p><ul><li>High Vacuum Drainage</li><li>Low Vacuum Drainage</li><li>Gravity Drainage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wound drainage sets are essential in postoperative care to remove fluid accumulation. High vacuum drainage utilizes negative pressure to efficiently evacuate excess fluids, promoting faster healing. Low vacuum drainage applies less pressure, often used for less invasive procedures, providing controlled fluid removal. Gravity drainage relies on gravitational force, allowing fluids to flow from the wound site into collection systems, suitable for simpler or less extensive surgeries. Each type serves distinct purposes, enhancing patient outcomes based on surgical needs.</p></p>
