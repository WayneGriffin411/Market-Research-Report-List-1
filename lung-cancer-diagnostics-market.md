<p><p>The lung cancer diagnostics market comprises various types of technologies aimed at detecting lung cancer effectively. Immunoassays utilize antibodies to detect specific proteins. Tissue arrays allow simultaneous analysis of multiple samples. Circulating tumor cells detect cancer cells in blood. Pharmacodiagnostics assist in personalized treatment plans. Biomarkers serve as indicators of disease presence or progression, along with other emerging diagnostic methods.</p></p>
