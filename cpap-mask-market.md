<p><strong>The CPAP Mask Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Nasal Pillow Mask</li><li>Nasal Mask</li><li>Full-face Mask</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CPAP mask market consists of three main types: nasal pillow masks, nasal masks, and full-face masks. Nasal pillow masks feature small cushions that fit directly into the nostrils, providing minimal contact and a lightweight feel. Nasal masks cover the nose entirely, offering a secure fit while allowing for easier breathing. Full-face masks cover both the nose and mouth, suitable for individuals who breathe through their mouths during sleep, ensuring a comprehensive and comfortable therapy experience.</p></p>
