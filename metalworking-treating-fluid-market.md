<p><p>The metalworking treating fluid market is primarily categorized into semi-synthetic and synthetic fluids. Semi-synthetic fluids combine mineral oil with synthetic additives, offering enhanced lubricity and cooling properties while maintaining a degree of biodegradability. They are versatile for various machining processes. In contrast, synthetic fluids are completely oil-free formulations made from chemical compounds, providing superior cooling and lubrication, reducing environmental impact, and minimizing Residue. Both types are essential for improving machining efficiency, tool life, and surface finish quality.</p></p>
