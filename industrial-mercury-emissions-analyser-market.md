<p><strong>The Industrial Mercury Emissions Analyser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption Mercury Analyzer</li><li>Cold Atomic Fluorescence Mercury Analyzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Mercury Emissions Analyzer market encompasses two primary types: Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption Mercury Analyzers and Cold Atomic Fluorescence Mercury Analyzers. Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption Analyzers detect mercury by converting it into a vapor and measuring its absorption of light, providing high sensitivity and reliable quantification. In contrast, Cold Atomic Fluorescence Analyzers utilize fluorescence properties of mercury atoms, offering enhanced sensitivity and lower detection limits. Both technologies are essential for monitoring mercury emissions in industrial settings to ensure environmental compliance and public safety.</p></p>
