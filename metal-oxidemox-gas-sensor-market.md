<p><p>Metal-Oxide (MOX) gas sensors are devices that detect the presence of various gases by utilizing the conductive properties of metal oxides. These sensors operate by analyzing changes in electrical resistance as gas molecules interact with a metal oxide layer, making them effective for detecting gases like carbon dioxide, methane, and volatile organic compounds.
