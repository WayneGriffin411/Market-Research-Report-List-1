<p><p>The heat-sensitive adhesive label market is categorized into two types: non-reversible and reversible. Non-reversible labels permanently bond to surfaces when activated by heat, making them ideal for tamper-evidence or long-lasting applications. In contrast, reversible labels can be removed and repositioned without damage, offering flexibility for temporary labeling needs. Each type serves distinct purposes, with non-reversible labels typically used for security or permanent identification, while reversible labels cater to dynamic labeling requirements in various industries.</p></p>
