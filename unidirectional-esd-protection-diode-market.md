<p><p>Unidirectional ESD Protection Diodes are semiconductor devices designed to protect electronic components from electrostatic discharge (ESD) events. Unlike their bidirectional counterparts, unidirectional ESD diodes allow current to flow in one direction, effectively safeguarding sensitive circuitry from voltage spikes caused by ESD. These diodes are essential in consumer electronics, automotive, telecommunications, and industrial applications, where the risk of ESD can cause significant damage to electronic systems.</p><p>The market for Unidirectional ESD Protection Diodes is anticipated to grow at a CAGR of 4.9% during the forecast period. Furthermore, advancements in materials technology are enabling the development of diodes with improved clamping voltage specifications, catering to diverse industry needs.
