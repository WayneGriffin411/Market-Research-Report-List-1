<p><p>The casting current transformer market is divided into two main types: measuring current transformers and protective current transformers. Measuring current transformers are designed to accurately measure electrical current for metering and monitoring applications, facilitating performance assessment and energy management. In contrast, protective current transformers focus on safeguarding electrical systems by detecting faults and ensuring safety through signal transmission to protective relays. Both types play critical roles in electrical distribution systems, enhancing efficiency and reliability in various industrial and commercial setups.</p></p>
