<p><p>Ethylene-Propylene-Diene Monomer (EPDM) is a synthetic rubber known for its excellent weather resistance and flexibility. The market for EPDM can be segmented into two primary types: solution polymerization and suspension polymerization. Solution polymerization involves dissolving monomers in a solvent to create a more uniform molecular structure, enhancing performance characteristics. In contrast, suspension polymerization involves dispersing monomers in a medium, leading to bulkier particles that can improve processing efficiency. Both methods cater to different requirements in applications across industries.</p></p>
