<p><p>The market for fans less than 225mm includes two primary types: axial fans and centrifugal fans. Axial fans move air parallel to the fan's axis, making them suitable for general ventilation and cooling applications due to their efficiency and compact size. Centrifugal fans, on the other hand, use a rotating mechanism to direct air perpendicularly to the intake, offering higher pressure and airflow, which is ideal for industrial settings and systems requiring greater resistance to airflow obstructions.</p></p>
