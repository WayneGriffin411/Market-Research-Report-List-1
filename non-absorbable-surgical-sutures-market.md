<p><p>The non-absorbable surgical sutures market encompasses various types, including polypropylene, nylon, stainless steel, and other specialty sutures. Polypropylene sutures are known for their durability and low tissue reactivity, making them ideal for soft tissue ligation. Nylon sutures offer excellent tensile strength and are used in skin closures. Stainless steel sutures provide maximum strength and are often used in orthopedic procedures. Other non-absorbable sutures include materials like silk and polyester, each selected based on specific surgical requirements and patient needs.</p></p>
