<p><p>The semiconductor process tapes market is primarily divided into UV tape and non-UV tape categories. UV tape is utilized for applications requiring precise adhesive properties and quick curing times under ultraviolet light, making it ideal for critical manufacturing processes. In contrast, non-UV tape serves applications where UV exposure is not suitable, providing reliable adhesion and thermal stability without the need for curing. Both types play vital roles in ensuring the integrity and performance of semiconductor devices during various fabrication stages.</p></p>
