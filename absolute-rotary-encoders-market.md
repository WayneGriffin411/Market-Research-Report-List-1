<p><p>Absolute Rotary Encoders are precision devices that measure the angular position of a rotating component, providing a unique and absolute value for each angle of rotation. Unlike incremental encoders, which only indicate movement from a reference point, absolute encoders maintain position information even when power is lost. <p><p>The absolute rotary encoders market is categorized into multi-turn and single-turn encoders. Single-turn encoders track position within a single rotation, providing precise angular data for applications with limited rotational movement. Conversely, multi-turn encoders measure the total number of rotations, maintaining accuracy over multiple turns and offering enhanced positional feedback for complex systems. Both types serve critical roles in automation, robotics, and industrial applications, ensuring reliable and accurate monitoring of rotational positions in various machinery.</p></p>
