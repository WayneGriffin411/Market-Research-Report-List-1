<p><strong>The Aviation Actuator Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric</li><li>Electromechanical</li><li>Electrohydraulic</li><li>Electrohydrostatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aviation actuator systems are critical for controlling various aircraft functions. Electric actuators use electric motors for precise movement, offering quick response and low maintenance. Electromechanical actuators combine mechanical components with electric motors, providing high reliability for flight control systems. Electrohydraulic actuators utilize hydraulic fluid for enhanced power and efficiency, ideal for large control surfaces. Electrohydrostatic actuators employ a compact, self-contained hydraulic system, delivering high force with minimal weight, making them suitable for modern aircraft applications where space and weight are crucial.</p></p>
