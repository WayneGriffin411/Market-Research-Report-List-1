<p><p>Animal plethysmography is a technique used to measure physiological parameters in animals, focusing on two main market types: body plethysmography and respiratory organ plethysmography. Body plethysmography assesses overall body volume and changes in mass during various activities, helping in studies of metabolism and growth. Conversely, respiratory organ plethysmography specifically examines breathing patterns, lung function, and gas exchange. Both methods are essential for research in veterinary medicine, pharmacology, and environmental biology, providing insights into animal health and physiology.</p></p>
