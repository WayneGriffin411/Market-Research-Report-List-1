<p><strong>The Instrument Landing System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Instrument Landing System CAT I</li><li>Instrument Landing System CAT II</li><li>Instrument Landing System CAT III</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Instrument Landing System (ILS) market is categorized into three main types: CAT I, CAT II, and CAT III. CAT I provides basic guidance with visibility minimums of 550 meters, suitable for most air traffic. CAT II offers enhanced accuracy with lower visibility requirements, allowing landings with a decision height as low as 100 feet. CAT III encompasses advanced systems enabling landings in very low visibility conditions, with automation levels that can eliminate the need for a pilot’s visual reference.</p></p>
