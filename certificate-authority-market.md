<p><strong>The Certificate Authority Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extended Validation</li><li>Organization Validation</li><li>Domain Validation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Certificate Authority (CA) market is categorized into three main types: Extended Validation (EV), Organization Validation (OV), and Domain Validation (DV). EV certificates provide the highest level of trust through rigorous vetting of the organization, displaying the company name in the browser. OV certificates authenticate the organization but with a less stringent validation process, while DV certificates verify domain ownership and are the quickest and easiest to obtain, making them suitable for basic identity assurance on websites.</p></p>
