<p><ul><li>Electrode Sheets</li><li>Electrode Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LNMO battery materials market comprises two primary types: electrode sheets and electrode powder. Electrode sheets are thin layers of active material that facilitate efficient electron and ion transport, crucial for the battery's overall performance. Conversely, electrode powder consists of finely milled materials used to produce electrode sheets, offering flexibility in manufacturing and allowing for tailored composite formulations. Both forms play essential roles in advancing lithium-sodium battery technologies, aiming for improved energy density, cycle life, and overall efficiency.</p></p>
