<p><p>The liquid tumor biopsy market encompasses two primary types: circulating tumor DNA (ctDNA) and circulating tumor cells (CTC). ctDNA involves the analysis of small fragments of tumor-derived genetic material found in the bloodstream, enabling insights into tumor dynamics and treatment responses. CTCs, on the other hand, are intact cancer cells shed from primary tumors into circulation, allowing for direct cellular analysis and characterization. Together, these methods enhance cancer diagnosis, monitoring, and personalized treatment strategies.</p></p>
