<p><ul><li>Wet Multiplate Clutches</li><li>Dry Single-Plate Clutches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The twin-clutch transmission market is categorized into wet multiplate clutches and dry single-plate clutches. Wet multiplate clutches use oil for lubrication and cooling, providing better performance and efficiency, making them suitable for high-performance applications. In contrast, dry single-plate clutches offer a simpler design, often resulting in reduced weight and space, which can enhance fuel efficiency in standard vehicles. Each type caters to different performance needs and vehicle applications, influencing their demand in the automotive industry.</p></p>
