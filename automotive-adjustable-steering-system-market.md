<p><p>The automotive adjustable steering system market includes two primary types: manually adjustable steering and electrically adjustable steering. In contrast, electrically adjustable steering systems use motors to enable precise adjustments with the push of a button, providing enhanced comfort and convenience. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences, improving driving ergonomics and overall vehicle experience.</p></p>
