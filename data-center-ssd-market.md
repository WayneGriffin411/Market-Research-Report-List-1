<p><p>The data center SSD market is primarily categorized into PCIe Gen4 and PCIe Gen5 types. PCIe Gen4 SSDs offer high-speed data transfer rates, suitable for applications requiring fast read/write capabilities but are limited by bandwidth compared to their successor. PCIe Gen5 SSDs, on the other hand, double the bandwidth of Gen4, enabling even faster data processing and lower latency, making them ideal for advanced workloads like AI and big data analytics. The ongoing shift toward Gen5 is driven by increasing performance demands.</p></p>
