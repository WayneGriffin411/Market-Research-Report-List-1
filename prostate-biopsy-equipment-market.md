<p><strong>The Prostate Biopsy Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TRUS (trans-rectal ultrasound)</li><li>TPBX (trans-perineal)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The prostate biopsy equipment market is primarily segmented into two types: TRUS (trans-rectal ultrasound) and TPBX (trans-perineal). TRUS utilizes ultrasound technology to guide needle placement through the rectal wall, allowing for targeted sampling of prostate tissue. In contrast, TPBX involves inserting the biopsy needle through the perineum, improving sampling accuracy and minimizing rectal complications. Both methods are essential for diagnosing prostate conditions, with each offering unique advantages in terms of patient comfort and procedural efficacy.</p></p>
