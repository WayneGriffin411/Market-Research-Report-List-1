<p><strong>The Luxury Passenger Car Tires Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bias Tire</li><li>Radial Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The luxury passenger car tire market primarily features two types: bias tires and radial tires. Bias tires have layers of fabric that run diagonally, providing a flexible sidewall and offering a smooth ride. However, they are less common in luxury cars due to their lower performance characteristics. Radial tires, on the other hand, feature steel belts that run perpendicular to the tread, providing better handling, improved fuel efficiency, and enhanced durability, making them the preferred choice for luxury vehicles.</p></p>
