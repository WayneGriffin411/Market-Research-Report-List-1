<p><p>The antimicrobial dressing market encompasses various types designed to enhance wound healing by preventing infection. Silver wound dressings utilize silver ions' antibacterial properties, effectively reducing microbial growth. Iodine antimicrobial wound dressings release iodine, combating a broad spectrum of pathogens. Honey antimicrobial wound dressings leverage honey's natural antibacterial and moisture-retentive qualities to promote healing and reduce inflammation. Polyhexamethylene biguanide (PHMB) dressings employ a synthetic polymer with strong antimicrobial effects, making them ideal for managing chronic and acute wounds.</p></p>
