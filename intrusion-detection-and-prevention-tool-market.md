<p><p>The Intrusion Detection and Prevention Tool market is categorized into two main types: Intrusion Detection Systems (IDS) and Intrusion Prevention Systems (IPS). IDS monitors network traffic for suspicious activity and alerts administrators of potential threats, emphasizing detection without immediate action. In contrast, IPS not only identifies threats but also actively prevents them by blocking harmful traffic in real-time. Together, these systems enhance organizational security by providing comprehensive surveillance and responsive measures against cyber threats.</p></p>
