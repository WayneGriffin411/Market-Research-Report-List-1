<p><strong>The Functional Dyspepsia Drug Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Prokinetic Drugs</li><li>Gastric Antisecretory Drugs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Functional Dyspepsia drug market consists mainly of prokinetic drugs and gastric antisecretory drugs. Prokinetic drugs enhance gastrointestinal motility, helping alleviate symptoms like bloating and delayed gastric emptying by promoting the movement of food through the digestive tract. Gastric antisecretory drugs, on the other hand, reduce stomach acid production, providing relief from discomfort associated with acid reflux and excessive gastric secretion. Together, these drug types address various symptoms of functional dyspepsia, improving patient quality of life.</p></p>
